# Cover Letter Quality

**Scoring rubric and thresholds are in `quality-gates.md` (section: Cover Letters & Proposals).**

This file contains only cover-letter-specific guidance not covered by the universal rubric.

## Target Lengths

- **Cover letter:** 1 page, 350--450 words
- **Proposal of academic work:** 2 pages body + 1 page references, 1000--1200 words

## Style Requirements

- **Language:** British English
- **Register:** Formal academic, confident and specific
- **Voice:** Active preferred ("I study..." not "My research is focused on...")
- **Specificity:** Every claim backed by a concrete example or name
