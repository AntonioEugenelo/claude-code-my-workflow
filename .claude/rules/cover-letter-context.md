## Style Guidelines

- **Language:** British English (Oxford-based applicant)
- **Register:** Formal academic, but not stiff -- confident and specific
- **Voice:** Active voice preferred ("I study..." not "My research is focused on...")
- **Specificity:** Every claim backed by a concrete example or name
- **Length:** 1 page (350--450 words) for cover letters; 2 pages body + 1 page references (~1000--1200 words) for proposals of academic work

## Tone Calibration

| Aim For | Avoid |
|---------|-------|
| Confident but not arrogant | "I am the ideal candidate" |
| Specific about research fit | Generic "world-class department" |
| Evidence-based claims | Unsubstantiated superlatives |
| Clear purpose for the visit | Vague "broaden horizons" |
| Professional warmth | Obsequious flattery |
| Concise, every sentence earns its place | Padding or filler paragraphs |
