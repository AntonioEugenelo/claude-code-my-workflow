# Referee Review Overlay

Use this overlay for referee reports, conference reviews, paper assessment forms, and structured review XML or web-form content.

## Rules

- Separate factual summary, major concerns, minor concerns, and recommendation.
- Be direct about identification, design, data, interpretation, contribution, and exposition weaknesses.
- Do not overstate certainty when the evidence in the paper is ambiguous.
- If a review form has fixed fields, satisfy the fields exactly before adding optional prose.
- Do not leak confidential review content into generic documentation or unrelated branches.

## Typical Capabilities

- read a submitted paper and draft a report;
- fill review forms or XML-style review files;
- score papers against a rubric;
- produce concise major/minor issue lists;
- check that criticism is actionable and fair.
